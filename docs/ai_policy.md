# AI Policy for Fast-F1

## Our Stance on AI-Generated Contributions

Fast-F1 welcomes contributions from everyone, including those who use AI tools for assistance. However, we have guidelines to ensure quality and maintain trust.

## When AI is Acceptable

You may use AI tools (like GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT, etc.) for:
- Getting explanations of code
- Learning how something works
- Fixing typos or simple formatting
- Generating boilerplate code (with careful review)

## When AI is NOT Acceptable

We will close PRs that:
- Are clearly written entirely by AI without human understanding
- Contain "AI slop" - generic comments, unnecessary changes, or code the submitter doesn't understand
- Fail to disclose AI usage when asked
- Are submitted just to get a "contribution" badge

## Disclosure Requirements

**If AI helped you write your PR, you MUST:**
1. Check the "AI Assisted" box in the PR template
2. Explain which parts AI helped with
3. Confirm you understand all the code you're submitting

## What Happens If You Don't Follow This

- First offense: PR closed with a note about our AI policy
- Repeated offenses: Your contributions may be blocked

## Why This Policy?

We want:
- High quality, maintainable code
- Contributors who understand their changes
- Respect for reviewers' time
- Genuine community contributions

## Questions?

If you're unsure whether your use of AI is acceptable, open a discussion or ask in the PR comments before submitting.

*This policy is adapted from the [NumPy AI Policy](https://numpy.org/devdocs/dev/ai_policy.html)*
